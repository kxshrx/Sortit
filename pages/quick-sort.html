Quick Sort

Quick Sort is a highly efficient, comparison-based sorting algorithm that uses a divide-and-conquer strategy. It works by selecting a 'pivot' element from the array and partitioning the other elements into two sub-arrays, according to whether they are less than or greater than the pivot. The sub-arrays are then sorted recursively. Quick Sort is known for its efficiency and is commonly used as a general-purpose sorting algorithm.
